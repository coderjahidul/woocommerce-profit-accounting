Complete Profit, Loss, COGS, Expenses, Monthly/Yearly Reports, Charts, CSV & PDF Export for WooCommerce.

== Description ==

**WooCommerce Profit & Accounting Manager** is a comprehensive tool designed to help WooCommerce store owners track their financial health. It provides detailed insights into revenue, Cost of Goods Sold (COGS), and business expenses.

Key Features:
* **Product Cost (COGS) Tracking**: Add cost price to each product and variation.
* **Expense Management**: Log and categorize business expenses.
* **Daily & Monthly Reports**: Detailed breakdown of profit and loss.
* **Interactive Charts**: Visualize your annual performance.
* **Export Options**: Download reports in CSV and PDF formats (Basic).
* **Modern UI**: Clean, responsive, and easy-to-use admin dashboard.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Start by adding cost prices to your products in the 'Pricing' section.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How is Profit calculated? =
Profit = Total Revenue - (Total COGS + Total Expenses).

= Where do I add product costs? =
Go to any product edit page -> General tab -> Pricing section. You will see a "Product Cost (COGS)" field.
